Born and Raised

I was born in Bakersfield CA.
And raised in a small town called Taft.

Hi, my name is Rudy and some of my passions are tennis, cybersecurity, and manga. I was pretty good at tennis and won a few awards no one cares about. In my free time, I love to draw manga (although I'm not good... Im getting better. I think..) and explore the latest trends in cybersecurity. I am determined to become a successful pen-tester one day, and I am always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow in this field. Thank you for taking the time to get to know me.

I used to attend UC Santa Barbara and was majoring in Biology, but I eventually dropped out. One of the biggest challenges I faced was the rigorous coursework in Chemistry. Let's just say that I struggled to see the "fun" in all those equations and experiments. But in the end, I realized that pursuing a career in Biology wasn't the right fit for me and decided to focus on other interests.

Just a little fun facts about me are that: I enjoy listening to the Dark Net Diaries podcast, a podcast that covers events in the world of cyberspace and the internet. I also have a love for Italian food, and I enjoy cooking in my free time. However, I must admit that I am not the best chef and would say that my cooking skills are "pasta" the point of being good. Despite my self-deprecating humor, I thank you for reaching this far to read about some dude's life. :,)

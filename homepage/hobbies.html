Manga & Video Games.

Berserk
Berserk is a dark fantasy manga series that follows the story of Guts, a skilled mercenary warrior who is haunted by his past and the demons that plague him. The series explores themes of camaraderie, loss, and the constant struggle against the evil that lurks in the world. Despite facing overwhelming odds, Guts remains determined to fight for his beliefs and protect those he holds dear. The series is known for its graphic violence and intense character development. Its philosophical and psychological elements, and its exploration of the human condition and the nature of good and evil...
Berserk has influenced the way I view the world and my own struggles, teaching me to persevere and be true to myself. It is a thought-provoking series that has left a lasting impact on me. Unfortunately, the writer of Berserk, Kentaro Miura, passed away before he was able to complete the story.
StarFox 64
StarFox 64 is a classic sci-fi action game released for the Nintendo 64 in 1997. It was revolutionary for its time due to it's plane flying 3rd person POV. You would fly in a space craft shooting other ships and enimies. It was pretty awesome. The game follows a team of anthropomorphic animals (my favorite being Fox the main character) as they defend the galaxy from the evil Andross and his army.
I remember constantly playing this game constantly as a child. I was fascinated by the diverse cast of characters and the different paths to beat the game, adding to its replay value. I enjoyed trying to beat the game using different routes and playing until the sun came up. To this day I place it in my top 5 games... OF ALL TIME!
The game had a lasting impact on me, as it sparked my love for science fiction and fast-paced action games. Overall, StarFox 64 is a timeless classic that holds a special place in my childhood memories.
Pokemon Platinum
I have been playing Pokemon throughout my entire life. It has been a constant presence in my gaming journey and has provided endless hours of entertainment and enjoyment.
Pokemon Platinum was my first Pokemon game. I remember getting my first handheld console, a Nintendo DS, and my parents taking my two older brothers and me to Best Buy to choose a handheld console. While my brothers chose the PSP, I stuck to my guns and chose the DS, which had a significant impact on my life. The DS introduced me to a whole new world of gaming and opened up a whole new avenue of entertainment for me. It was a defining moment in my life and one that I will always remember fondly.
